About Us

Anviksha 2.0 is not just a techfest — it’s a convergence of ideas, creativity, and innovation. Hosted by the passionate students of NMIMS Chandigarh, Anviksha brings together the curious, the inventive, and the bold under one banner.

After the success of its inaugural edition, Anviksha returns bigger, brighter, and bolder. With a rich lineup of technical events, creative design challenges, gaming tournaments and cultural showdowns, we aim to celebrate not just technology, but the spirit of collaboration and exploration.

From coding battles to design wars, robotics challenges to e-sports arenas, every event is crafted to ignite minds and push boundaries. Whether you’re a coder, a creator, or just curious — there’s a space for you here.

At its heart, Anviksha 2.0 is a student-led celebration of future tech and fearless thinking — backed by a dedicated team, supported by mentors, and powered by passion.
